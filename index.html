What it does

A quiet safety layer for Word AutoRecover.

Watches your recovery folder

Point Microsoft Word and Word History at the same AutoRecover folder. The app monitors that folder for recoverable document artifacts.

Stores immutable snapshots

Captured versions are copied into Word History's local storage so later Word cleanup or overwrites do not erase the record.

Restores when needed

Use a normal copy restore or recover through Word when you need to inspect or revive an earlier AutoRecover version.

Privacy first

Designed to stay on your Mac.

Word History does not require an account, cloud sync, or a remote document service. Its job is to preserve local Word AutoRecover history in local app storage.

The recommended setup uses a user-managed folder, usually ~/Documents/WordAutorecovery, so Word and Word History share a clear, user-controlled location.
