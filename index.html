Shared Pattern

Built for the messy middle between good ideas and real tools.

These apps are intentionally close to the systems people already use: Microsoft Word, EndNote, Home Assistant, GitHub, and local macOS storage. They do not ask users to rebuild their whole workflow before they can solve one practical problem.

Each project keeps a visible path back to its source, support notes, or download surface so the public site can stay a stable front door while the apps keep moving.
